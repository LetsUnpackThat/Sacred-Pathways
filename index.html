Discovering Your Spiritual Temperament

How do you draw closest to God? Through quiet contemplation, active service, intellectual study, or celebration? Each person connects with God in unique ways.

This assessment will help you identify your spiritual temperament among nine sacred pathways. By understanding how you naturally connect with God, you can develop practices that nurture your faith and deepen your relationship with the Divine.

The assessment contains 54 questions. For each statement, rate how true it is for you on a scale from 1 to 5, where 1 means "Not true at all" and 5 means "Very true." We encourage you to avoid using 3 - push yourself toward a 2 or 4 to get clearer results.

Take your time and answer honestly. There are no right or wrong answers, only your authentic responses.
